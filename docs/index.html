Cue

Save. Remember. Learn.
A personal AI tool that captures what you save across platforms, organizes it, and brings back what matters.

The problem

We save things everywhere — screenshots, articles, voice notes, random ideas. None of it connects. All of it gets forgotten.

How it works

📥

Capture

Share anything to a Telegram bot. Screenshots, links, voice notes, text. It classifies and stores automatically.

🔔

Resurface

Daily nudges based on interest and goal alignment. Nothing sits unseen for more than 7 days.

🔗

Connect

Weekly digest groups related saves from different platforms into themes using semantic similarity.

Demo

Features

📸 Multi-format capture

Screenshots, URLs, voice notes, plain text — each processed by a format-specific extractor, then classified by one universal AI classifier.

🎯 Smart resurfacing

Lifecycle-based nudges: guaranteed first look within 7 days, priority rotation with cooldowns, and forced decisions after 3 ignores.

🔗 Cross-source clustering

HDBSCAN groups related saves from different platforms into themes — no manual tagging, no fixed number of clusters.

🧠 Go Deep

Tap one button and get a category-aware AI analysis in your weekly digest — skill breakdowns for job posts, argument summaries for articles, feasibility checks for ideas.

📬 Weekly digest

Saturday email with themed clusters, article summaries, and deep dives. Sunday follow-up for anything still pending.

⏰ Configurable schedule

Set your own nudge and reminder times. Rate interest and goal alignment at save time — or skip it, defaults apply.
